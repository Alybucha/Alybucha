This semester will be the start of my of my third year at the University of New Mexico. I am majoring in Multimedia Journalism. With my family owning a sign shop, I was able to gain some background knowledge about graphic designing. So when I came to UNM I wanted to focus on car graphic designing. I thought I would like to one day own my own graphic design company that focuses on car graphics.

Since attending my Fall 2022 semester at UNM I have realized I have grown a passion for Investigative Journalism. Each time I am approached with an assignment I always liked to find the mystery stories that took a little extra thinking. I appreciate the knowledge I have learned this semester as it has really made me evaluate what I would like to do long term.
